Interactive Workout Guide for Ramadan

Weekly Routine

Day Workout Focus Timing
Monday Full-Body Strength + Core Night (Post-Taraweeh)
Tuesday Cardio (Low-Impact) + Stretching Pre-Iftar
Wednesday Upper Body (Push-Ups, Shoulders) + Core Night (Post-Taraweeh)
Thursday Lower Body (Legs, Glutes) Night (Post-Taraweeh)
Friday Rest or Light Walking -
Saturday Full-Body Strength + Core Night (Post-Taraweeh)
Sunday Cardio (Jump Rope, Walking) + Core Pre-Iftar

Full-Body Strength (Monday & Saturday)

Warm-Up (5 minutes)

Arm Circles – 30 seconds

High Knee March – 30 seconds

Shoulder Rolls – 30 seconds

Bodyweight Squats – 10 reps

Main Workout (3 Rounds)

Bodyweight Squats – 15 reps

Push-Ups (Knee or Regular) – 10 reps

Glute Bridges – 15 reps

Plank – 30 seconds

Superman Hold – 30 seconds

Cool-Down (5 minutes)

Cat-Cow Stretch

Child’s Pose

Hamstring Stretch

Cardio & Core (Tuesday & Sunday)

Warm-Up (5 minutes)

Arm Swings – 30 seconds

Torso Twists – 30 seconds

Light Jogging in Place – 30 seconds

Main Workout (3 Rounds)

Jump Rope (or Jogging in Place) – 30 seconds

Mountain Climbers (Slow Pace) – 30 seconds

Leg Raises – 10 reps

Side Plank (Each Side) – 20 seconds

Cool-Down (5 minutes)

Hip Flexor Stretch

Shoulder Stretch

Upper Body Strength (Wednesday)

Warm-Up (5 minutes)

Shoulder Circles – 30 seconds

Main Workout (3 Rounds)

Incline Push-Ups (Hands on a Chair/Wall) – 12 reps

Triceps Dips (On a Sturdy Chair) – 10 reps

Pike Push-Ups (Shoulders Focused) – 10 reps

Superman Hold (Back & Core) – 30 seconds

Cool-Down (5 minutes)

Chest Stretch

Triceps Stretch

Lower Body Strength (Thursday)

Warm-Up (5 minutes)

Leg Swings – 30 seconds

Calf Raises – 15 reps

Main Workout (3 Rounds)

Bodyweight Squats – 15 reps

Lunges (Alternating) – 10 reps per leg

Glute Bridges – 15 reps

Calf Raises – 15 reps

Wall Sit – 30 seconds

Cool-Down (5 minutes)

Hamstring Stretch

Quad Stretch

Rest or Light Walking (Friday)

Take a 30-minute light walk or choose your favorite route. Use any of the cool-down stretches above as needed.
